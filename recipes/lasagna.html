Vegetarian lasagna

go back to all recipes

If you follow the recipe, you can eat a delicious lasagna just like the one on the picture

In this recipe, you will combine a few wonderful vegetables with the beauty of lasagana pasta to create an absolutly fabouly dish.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • mushrooms
  • bell pepper
  • lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente.
  2. Bake, uncovered, for 40 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
