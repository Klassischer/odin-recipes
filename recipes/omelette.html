Breakfast omelette

go back to all recipes

Do you also get hungry seeing this picture?

If you follow the recipe, you can eat a delicious breakfast omelette.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • butter
  • spinach
  • mushrooms
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the eggs in a pan and stir for a few minutes.
  2. Remove all contents of the pan and put aside
  3. Crack open eggs and combine in the pan for a smooth layer
  4. let it harden and top the veggies on it in the end
