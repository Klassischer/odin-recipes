Mac and cheese

Do you also get hungry seeing this picture?

If you follow the recipe, you can eat a delicious mac and cheese including some healthy broccoli

Ingredients

  • maccaroni
  • butter
  • cheddar cheese
  • broccoli

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Pour cheese mixture over macaroni.
